Qomon is an application developed to help couples or small groups keep track of their common expenses.

If you and your partner / roommate / friend / collegue spend money on common items such as groceries, gifts, utility bills, etc., but you use your own money to pay for them, you may want to know how much you owe each other at the end of the month. This is where Qomon comes in.

You can add up to two partners! Just add your partner's email on the settings page, ask your partner to add yours, et Voilà.

Qomon was developed by Javiera Guedes, feel free to send her your comments or questions to this email address.
